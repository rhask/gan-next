Cloudflare Zaraz

Like Google's new server-sided tag management evolution, Cloudflare Zaraz executes client-side code on the server. This gives us very granular control over the flow of data (including PII) and allows us as a marketing team to participate in fully compliant practices without sacrificing functionality or analytic insights.

Crucially, Zaraz will also circumvent tracking blockers. Many visitors block Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics from running in the browser. In fact, greater than 40% of visitors block any and all forms of tracking, creating a disparity in campaign attribution versus actual conversions and pipeline development. Implementing Zaraz unlocks data from nearly all visitors, enables granular data compliance, and vastly decreases time to first contentful paint.
